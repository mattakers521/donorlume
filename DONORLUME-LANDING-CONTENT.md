## HOW IT WORKS SECTION

**Section heading:**
Three modules. One platform. Zero busywork.

**Module 1: Prospect Discovery**
Heading: Find your next major gift.
Body: Search millions of IRS 990 filings to discover foundations and organizations giving to causes like yours. See their revenue, assets, grant history, and filing PDFs — all in one place. No more Googling foundation names and squinting at tax documents. Save the best matches to your pipeline and track them from first discovery to first gift.

**Module 2: Lapsed Donor Scoring**
Heading: Know exactly who to call first.
Body: Upload your donor list from any CRM. Our RFM+ scoring engine analyzes every lapsed donor across four dimensions — recency, frequency, monetary value, and tenure — to give each one a reactivation score from 0 to 100. See at a glance who's still giving to other organizations, who's gone cold, and who's most likely to come back with the right outreach.

**Module 3: AI Outreach Studio**
Heading: Send emails that sound like you spent an hour. In seconds.
Body: Select donors from your scored list and generate deeply personalized reactivation emails powered by AI. Each email references the donor's actual giving history, relationship notes, and your organization's mission. Choose your tone, customize your ask, edit any draft before sending. Walk into your next board meeting with a real outreach pipeline — not a spreadsheet of good intentions.

## FAQ SECTION

**Section heading:**
Questions? We've got answers.

**Q: What is DonorLume?**
A: DonorLume is a donor intelligence platform that helps fundraisers at nonprofits and charities discover new prospects, score lapsed donors for reactivation, and generate personalized outreach — all powered by public data and AI.

**Q: Who is DonorLume built for?**
A: Development Directors, Fundraising Directors, Executive Directors, and anyone responsible for raising money at a nonprofit, charity, or fundraising organization. It's designed specifically for small to mid-size organizations that don't have a dedicated prospect research team.

**Q: Where does the prospect data come from?**
A: Prospect Discovery searches IRS 990 and 990-PF filings through the ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer — a publicly available, free database of nonprofit tax filings. We don't scrape, buy, or broker personal data. Every piece of data in Prospect Discovery is public record.

**Q: Can I upload my donor list from my CRM?**
A: Yes. Export a CSV from any CRM — Bloomerang, Salesforce Nonprofit, Little Green Light, Kindful, Neon CRM, DonorPerfect, or any system that can export a spreadsheet. DonorLume auto-detects your column names, so you don't need to reformat anything.

**Q: How does the AI outreach work?**
A: When you select donors and click "Generate," DonorLume sends each donor's profile (giving history, relationship notes, lapsed duration) to Claude AI, which writes a unique, personalized email for each person. You review, edit, and approve every draft before it goes anywhere. The AI never sends emails on its own.

**Q: Is my donor data secure?**
A: Yes. Every organization's data is completely isolated in our database — no other user or organization can see your donors, prospects, or outreach. All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest. We never sell, share, or use your data for any purpose other than running DonorLume for your organization.

**Q: Who owns my data?**
A: You do. Always. Vibrant Causes is a data processor, not a data owner. You can export your data at any time, and if you cancel your account, you can request full deletion.

**Q: Can I cancel at any time?**
A: Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no contracts and no cancellation fees. If you cancel, your account remains active until the end of your billing period, then downgrades to the free tier. Your data is preserved — we never delete it unless you ask us to.

**Q: Do I need to install anything?**
A: No. DonorLume is a web application that runs in your browser. There's nothing to download or install. It works on Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge on both desktop and mobile.

**Q: How much does the AI outreach cost beyond my plan limit?**
A: Each plan includes a set number of AI-generated emails per month. If you need more, you can upgrade to a higher plan. We don't charge per-email overage fees — you always know exactly what you're paying.

**Q: I'm a consultant who works with multiple nonprofits. Can I use DonorLume?**
A: Absolutely. Each nonprofit you work with would have their own organization account with isolated data. Our Growth and Enterprise plans support this workflow. Contact us if you need a consultant-specific setup.
